Top 20 Shiva Temples in the World

These sacred temples represent devotion, history, architectural brilliance, and the eternal spiritual energy of Lord Shiva.

Sacred Shiva Temples

Click on any temple image to view its exact location on Google Maps.

1. Kashi Vishwanath Temple

Varanasi, Uttar Pradesh, India

One of the twelve Jyotirlingas, believed to grant moksha and eternal liberation.

2. Kedarnath Temple

Uttarakhand, India

Nestled in the Himalayas, Kedarnath is a Jyotirlinga associated with the Pandavas and represents immense spiritual endurance.

3. Somnath Temple

Gujarat, India

The first Jyotirlinga, symbolizing Shiva‚Äôs eternal presence. Rebuilt several times, it stands as a symbol of resilience.

4. Mahakaleshwar Temple

Ujjain, Madhya Pradesh, India

Famous for the unique Bhasma Aarti, Mahakaleshwar is a Jyotirlinga where Lord Shiva is worshipped as the Lord of Time.

5. Rameshwaram Temple

Tamil Nadu, India

Built by Lord Rama, this Jyotirlinga is known for the longest temple corridors and sacred theerthams.

6. Trimbakeshwar Temple

Nashik, Maharashtra, India

Origin of the sacred Godavari River, this Jyotirlinga features a unique three-faced lingam.

7. Omkareshwar Temple

Madhya Pradesh, India

Located on an Om-shaped island in the Narmada River, Omkareshwar is a powerful Jyotirlinga.

8. Mallikarjuna Temple

Srisailam, Andhra Pradesh, India

A Jyotirlinga and Shakti Peetha together, located in the scenic Nallamala forests.

9. Bhimashankar Temple

Maharashtra, India

Surrounded by dense forests, this Jyotirlinga is associated with the legend of demon Bhima.

10. Vaidyanath Temple

Deoghar, Jharkhand, India

Known as the healer Jyotirlinga, worshipped by Ravana for immortality.

11. Nageshwar Temple

Gujarat, India

This Jyotirlinga symbolizes protection from poison and negative forces.

12. Grishneshwar Temple

Ellora, Maharashtra, India

The twelfth Jyotirlinga, located near the UNESCO-listed Ellora Caves.

13. Pashupatinath Temple

Kathmandu, Nepal

The most sacred Shiva temple outside India, located on the banks of the Bagmati River.

14. Amarnath Cave Temple

Jammu & Kashmir, India

Famous for the naturally forming ice lingam, accessible only during a sacred pilgrimage.

15. Lingaraj Temple

Odisha, India

A magnificent ancient temple showcasing classic Kalinga architecture.

16. Brihadeeswarar Temple

Thanjavur, Tamil Nadu, India

A UNESCO World Heritage Site built by Raja Raja Chola with a massive Shiva lingam.

17. Kailasanathar Temple

Kanchipuram, Tamil Nadu, India

One of the oldest stone temples in South India, dedicated to Lord Shiva.

18. Baijnath Temple

Himachal Pradesh, India

A serene Himalayan temple known for its ancient stone architecture.

19. Murudeshwar Temple

Karnataka, India

Famous for the towering 123-foot Shiva statue overlooking the Arabian Sea.
